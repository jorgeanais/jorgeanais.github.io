I'm Jorge Anais, a master's student in Astronomy at CITEVA in Antofagasta, Chile. My thesis is about the search and characterization of stellar clusters in near-infrared data using unsupervised machine learning algorithms. I'm also working as a research assistant for a project about modeling exo-planetary atmospheres. One of the things that I love most as an astronomer is going to observe, that is why I am also working as an observer at Las Campanas Observatory for the survey 1M2H.

During work, I spend most of my time reading papers and programming. In my free time, I enjoy outdoor activities like trekking and riding a bicycle. Music and video-games are my favorite hobbies.

I am a proud father of an adorable boy and I have a weakness for nerdy things.
